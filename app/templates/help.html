Help & Support – BOB Bank

Welcome to the BOB Bank Help Center. Here you'll find answers to common questions and guidance for using our services smoothly.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

🔐 How do I create a new bank account?

Go to the "Create Account" page, fill out your details, and click submit. Your account will be created instantly.

💳 How do I generate a PIN?

Use the "Generate PIN" section. Enter your account number and follow the instructions to securely create your PIN.

💰 How do I deposit or withdraw money?

Navigate to "Deposit" or "Withdraw" from the main menu. Enter the amount and confirm your action.

🔁 How do I transfer funds to another account?

Go to the "Account Transfer" section, enter the receiver's account number and amount, then click Transfer.

📊 How do I check my balance?

Click on "Balance Enquiry" and enter your account number. Your current balance will be displayed securely.

❌ How can I delete my account?

In the "Delete Account" section, enter your account credentials and follow the confirmation steps.
